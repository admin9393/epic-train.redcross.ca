Good news for travelers. Dubai visa will be available in just five days. UAE authorities have recently reduced the processing time for work visas and residency visas.<\/p>\n

It used to take 30 days for Dubai visa processing earlier.<\/p>\n

Now that time has been reduced to just five days. Apart from this, as per the new guidelines, instead of 16 documents, now only five documents will be required. Dubai’s Ruler and Vice President Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid announced the plan last Wednesday.
\nReferring to the inauguration of the employment package, Mohammed bin Rashid said, the project is designed to speed up, simplify and streamline the residency and employment processes.<\/p>\n

The new scheme will deal with services such as issuing work permits for employees and new employees, residency permit renewals, physical examinations for residency visas, work permit cancellations and Emirates ID fingerprint scans.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
