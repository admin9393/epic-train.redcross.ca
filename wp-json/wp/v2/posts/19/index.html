Almighty Allah has declared mankind as the best of creation, half of which is women. The status of women in Islam is immense. In the Holy Qur’an, there are discussions about different women in different contexts. Some of whom are wives of prophets, some are mothers of prophets.<\/p>\n

Today we will try to know briefly about the women discussed in the Holy Quran, Inshallah.
\nHawa (A.): Hawa (A.) is the mother of mankind. Consort of Adam (A.S.). His discussion has come in the Holy Qur’an.<\/p>\n

It was narrated, ‘And I said, O Adam, dwell thou and thy wife in Paradise, and eat of it at ease, according to thy will, and do not approach this tree, lest thou be among the wrongdoers.’ (Surah: Baqarah, Verses: 35-36)
\nSarah (A.S.): She is one of the wives of Ibrahim (A.S.). The Holy Qur’an mentions the fact that God Almighty gave him the good news of giving him a child. It is said, ‘And his wife was standing, he laughed.<\/p>\n

Then I gave him the glad tidings of Isaac and after Isaac of Jacob.’ (Surah: Hud, verse: 71)
\nHajar (A.S.): She is also a wife of Ibrahim (A.S.), mother of Ishmael (A.S.). On the instructions of Almighty Allah, Ibrahim (A.S.) left him in the uninhabited valley or Kaaba courtyard. The incident is mentioned in the Holy Qur’an, which also indirectly mentions Hagar. It has been said, ‘O our Lord, I have indeed settled some of my progeny in a barren valley near Your Sacred House, O our Lord, so that they may establish the prayer.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
