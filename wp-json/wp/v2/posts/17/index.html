The importance of Friday is immense. Friday is the weekly Eid for Muslims. The importance and significance of Juma is so great that a separate Surah named ‘Juma’ has been revealed in the Qur’an. It is mentioned in Surah Juma of the Qur’an: ‘O believers! When the call to prayer is called on the day of Friday, proceed to the remembrance of Allah (to the mosque) and leave off (all worldly activities).<\/p>\n

It is good for you, if you only knew.’ (Surah: Juma, verse: 9)
\nJuma’s Day<\/p>\n

The most important act of Friday is to perform Friday prayers with great importance. This is the most virtuous act of Friday. Many virtues of Friday prayers have been described in the hadith.<\/p>\n

Friday prayers are forgiven<\/p>\n

Humans are prone to sin. According to the hadith, the sins of the previous week are forgiven through Friday prayers. Abu Huraira (RA) said, Rasulullah (SAW) said, \u201cWhoever takes a bath on Friday, wears good clothes and uses perfume if he has one, then comes to the Friday prayer and does not go forward with his head over other worshippers. Performs prescribed prayers. Then the imam remains silent from the time he comes out for the khutbah until the salam, then this deed of his will be expiation for all his sins from the previous Friday to the next Friday.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
