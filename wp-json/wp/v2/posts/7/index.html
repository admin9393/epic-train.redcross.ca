Women workers are increasing in the banking sector.\u00a0Employment of women in private and specialized banks has increased significantly.\u00a0According to the latest data of Bangladesh Bank, by the end of 2023, the number of women workers in the country’s banking sector has increased to 33 thousand 346 people.\u00a0Whereas at the end of 2022, this number was 31 thousand 939 people.<\/p>\n

Accordingly, the employment of women in the banking sector has increased.\u00a0At present, the rate of female officers in the banking sector is 16.37 percent as compared to males.<\/p>\n

On the other hand, 9 foreign commercial banks have the least number of female officers-employees (981 people), but the rate of female officers-employees in foreign commercial banks is higher than other banks, 24.18 percent.<\/p>\n

Bankers reported that women’s participation in education has increased significantly.<\/p>\n
