Saudi Arabia has issued new instructions on the Tawaf of the Holy Kaaba on the occasion of the upcoming month of Ramadan. According to the new guidelines, only those performing Umrah can circumambulate the Holy Kaaba. And ordinary Muslims are asked to pray inside the mosque. This initiative is taken to ensure the safety and comfort of Umrah pilgrims.<\/p>\n

This information was given in a statement by the General Presidency Department, which manages the Holy Mosque.
\nEntry into the Holy Kaaba premises was strictly controlled since the Corona period. Only those performing Umrah could enter there. No one could enter there if he wanted to perform Tawaf normally.<\/p>\n

And according to the new instructions, now the worshipers will not be able to pray in front of the Kaaba. Many people think that such an initiative has been taken to control the overflowing crowd of Umrah pilgrims in the upcoming month of Ramadan.
\nMeanwhile, one imam has been reassigned to the Holy Masjid al-Haram and two to the Prophet’s Mosque. They are Dr. Yasir Bin Rashid Al-Dawsari, Dr. Khalid Bin Sulaiman Al-Muhanna and Dr. Ahmad Bin Ali Al-Huzayfi.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
