The financial services company City Index has published the list of countries according to the number of female billionaires around the International Women’s Day on March 8.<\/p>\n

Forbes Magazine’s Live Billionaire Tracker has been used to compile this list.
\nThe United States is on the first place in this list. The number of female billionaires in the country is 97. China is second in the list.<\/p>\n

\nIndia’s women billionaires are 15 in fifth place. Australia, Switzerland and Brazil have 9 people in sixth place. Hong Kong, Spain, Sweden and France each have seven people in seventh place.
